How to Play

1) Click on one of the six             on Google Maps.
2) Once you click on one of the red flags, press                   and you will be instantly taken to that point of the map.
3) Get onto the bike and happy pedaling!

How to operate the bike:

Please do not jump off the bike while in motion, as you can hurt yourself, or the people around you

Getting on the bike:
1) Place one foot on the pedal, and then use that to step onto and sit onto the seat.
2) Once you are on the bike, you can then place your other foot into the other pedal. Make sure your feet are strapped in or snug in the pedals so that you can get as much traction as possible.
3) Pedal for a bit on the bike, and raise the difficulty of friction accordingly using the black knob on the bike.
Getting off the bike:
1) Make sure you have fully stopped.
2) Unstrap or take your feet off the bike.
3) Use one of your legs to carefully get off the bike on one side.

Seat Adjustment

To prevent injury and a more comfortable ride, it is important to adjust your seat to where you feel comfortable

Pull out the knob below your seat and adjusting the seat up or down. Push knob back in and tighten the knob.

Parts of the Bike

Handlebars

Use this to help you have something to grab onto to when you want to go faster or to rest if you're tired

Brakes

Located under the seat, in the middle of the bike, press the lever to slow down

Pedals

Located under the seat, attached onto two metal bars, use these to start moving the bike forward

Seat

Sit here and start pedaling!

Buttons

Located on the handlebars, press right or left to turn on the map
