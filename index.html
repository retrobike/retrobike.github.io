Raspberry Pi Tutorial

What You Will Need

Steps

Before you get on the bike, make sure your seat is adjusted

To prevent injury and a more comfortable ride, it is important to adjust your seat to where you feel comfortable

Pull out the knob below your seat and adjusting the seat up or down. Push knob back in and tighten the knob.

You want to make sure that your leg is slightly bent as shown


To get on the bike:

Place one foot on the pedal, and then use that to step onto the sit on the seat.

Once you are on the bike, you can then place your other foot into the other pedal. Make sure your feet are strapped in or snug in the pedals so that you can get as much traction as possible.

Pedal for a bit on the bike, and raise the difficulty of friction accordingly using the black knob on the bike.


Getting off the bike can be extremely dangerous if done wrong. Make sure you follow these instructions closely so that you donâ€™t get hurt:

Make sure the wheels have fully stopped by pressing down on the brake.

Unstrap or take your feet off the bike

Use one of your legs to carefully get off the bike on one side


Next, I will explain how to connect the Raspberry Pi onto the TV and how to turn it on

Plug in the AV out cord, the blue cord with #1 on it, into the TV

  • The red plug should go in the yellow, while the yellow goes in the red. White will just go into white.
  • Make sure you are on the right component/channel.

Plug in the controller into the usb port, labeled #2 in the color purple.

Plug cord #3 into the battery, which is in the color red. This will turn on the RPI.


How to start playing

Choose your console by pressing left/right and press a to select it

Choose the game you would like to play and then press a

To save, follow the save instructions in game

To quit, press (select) and (start) at the same time

To turn off Raspberry Pi, first make sure you are at the home page and then unplug cord #3 (red) from the battery (red). If you plug the cord when you are not in the home page, your game will not save.
